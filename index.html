CXC, CSEC and CAPE programme

(Caribbean Examinations Council) is the regional body responsible for examinations in the caribbean.

CSEC (Caribbean Secondary Education Certificate) examination are taken at the end of fifth form are required for graduation.

CAPE/strong> (caribbean Advanced Proficiency Examination) is taken in sixth Form and prepare students for tertiary education.
